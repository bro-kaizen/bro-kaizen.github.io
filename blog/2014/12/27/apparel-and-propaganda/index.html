Apparel and Propaganda

Dec 27th, 2014 3:36 pm

The human animal is by and large easily bamboozled by the surface impressions of things. You will meet comparatively few people who see beyond the most superficial of apperances.

For the technically inclined—those who are used to examining pairs of manifolds with wildly divergent appearances that are nonetheless provably topologically equivalent—this is profoundly annoying. “Why can’t these stupid people see past shallow surface appearances and get to the real meat of the topic!?”
