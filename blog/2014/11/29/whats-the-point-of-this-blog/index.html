What’s the Point of This Blog?

Nov 29th, 2014 7:48 pm

This blog serves a couple purposes.

  • I’m hoping to get new ideas by writing short essays, following Paul Graham’s (persuasive) claim that

“Writing doesn’t just communicate ideas; it generates them. If you’re bad at writing and don’t like to do it, you’ll miss out on most of the ideas writing would have generated.”

  • I have strong opinions about a variety of topics, including but not limited to self improvement, learning techniques, civil society, and masculinity. In general, these topics are either boring or not socially acceptable in polite company, so I never get to discuss them. Maybe we can fix that here.
